Web Application

TrackTruck

Tracking system for the biggest local gas-importing company. Tracks every charge like driver's salary, fuel charges, and other extra charges. Keeps every detail from empty and full weights both at the starting point and destination to driver's name and license data. Reminders for license renewal and technical maintenance. After all, at the end of the trip(when the truck goes abroad and returns with gas) program counts all costs.

Automation system

GasSystem

All-automated system for the biggest local gas station network. Real-time info with Socket.io about every liter of sold gas in all stations and all pumps, weekly, monthly and yearly reports. Real-time graphs of earnings, and sold gas. Day and night shift tracking. Long story short, everything that business needs.

Real-time chat

Chat App

Real-time chat application with room system and comfy UI. There are 2 types of rooms: public and private. Anyone can join a public room but to join private, you need the room's name. You can easily invite your friend to the room by link sharing option. There's also a random matching mode that allows you to talk to random strangers with a nickname chosen by you. Technologies used: Node.js, Express.js, Socket.io..
